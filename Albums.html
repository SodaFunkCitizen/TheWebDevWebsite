The Age of Plastic (1980)

The Buggles' debut album, featuring the iconic hit "Video Killed the Radio Star." A pioneering synth-pop album, it set the stage for the 1980s sound with futuristic themes and electronic production.

Adventures in Modern Recording (1981)

The band's second and final album, featuring a more experimental and sophisticated sound. It delves into new wave and electronic influences, showcasing the evolution of The Buggles' music.
